VR Experience, 2020
EXHIBItion
ITP/IMA Spring Show 2020
Toolkit
Unity / Photogrammetry
Music is from the internet.
VR Confinement Sketches is a VR video experience presenting the creators’ lives during the COVID-19 pandemic. Through scanning real objects in the confinement spaces (usually in our home), a virtual space combining real-life objects and simple models is built to illustrate individual experiences to the audience.
