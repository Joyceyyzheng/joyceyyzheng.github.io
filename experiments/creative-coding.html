Mirror

An interactive web space using poseNet and THREE.js.
The matter of existence has long been a question. What are we? Are we composed of and rely on our skins, organs, bodies, or are we only our core: our soul? By using mirror as the representation, this project aims to reveal the relationship between people in reality and their virtual identities in their own reflections.
