O

PC Game, 2020
In collaboration with Tian Qin & Zhao Yang
Responsible for Everything but hard programming
Toolkit
Unity / Photoshop
*Most models & Music is from the internet.
O is a PC-side first person puzzle game. The player is in a circular corridor, by observing and exploring the scene and interacting with the objects inside, the player will find out the secret in the room and escape from it.
