Human Relic is an 10-minute VR experience that brings the audience back to the wilderness where they can explore a distinct, grandiose relic. Truth of the world will be challenged, explored, and revealed during the journey. How far has the world been evolved? What will happen next? During the experience, audiences will be guided into a magical tour where they are able to discover and interact inside the relic, to uncover the truth under the huge cave. Indoor, outdoor scenes and interactable objects are designed to better illustrate the story, help people reflect on themselves in a virtual era where human beings extinct with an identity of a future creature.
