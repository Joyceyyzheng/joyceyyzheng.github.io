Ocean Cage

Ocean cage is an wearable accessories & installation made by recycled plastics, aiming to evoke people’s awareness on ocean environment protecting. By designing a set of accessories of earrings and necklaces, the designer wants to illustrate that the ocean animals represented by dolphins and whales have been suffering the plastic pollution in the ocean severely.
‍
Toolkit: Illustrator | Laser Cut | Fuse Machine



Bubble!

Bubble is a ragdoll cat who loves drinking bubble tea the designer made for her friend as a personalized toy. There are 4 back-up bubble teas in different favors based on the bubble teas they have had together, and the player can switch the bubble tea in Bubble’s hat every day.

Toolkit: Cinema 4D | Photoshop | Illustrator | 3D printing
