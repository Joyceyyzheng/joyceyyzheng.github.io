Revealing is an endlessly interactive experiential installation that aims to evoke the construction of contemporary people's awareness of their own existence. With the advent of the new era and the influx of information, everyone is swept up in the whirlpool of information, constantly and passively receiving a cacophony of outside voices. People often find it difficult to give voice to themselves as self-subjects, and thus gradually lose the ability to express themselves. Through the experience, people will contemplate the complementarity between human and technology, and discover their own existence through a dialogue with their artistic selves. The audience will reveal their own voice through the ever-changing figure, allowing themselves to be heard and felt.
