Revealing

Interactive Installation, 2020
Screen-based, Projected with Portable Monitor
1.5m x 1.5m x 2m
Exhibition
NYU Shanghai IMA Show Fall 2020
Future Lab 2021, Shanghai
Toolkit
Creative coding: P5.JS / ML5.JS
Revealing is an endlessly interactive experiential installation that aims to evoke the construction of contemporary people's awareness of their own existence. With the advent of the new era and the influx of information, everyone is swept up in the whirlpool of information, constantly and passively receiving a cacophony of outside voices. People often find it difficult to give voice to themselves as self-subjects, and thus gradually lose the ability to express themselves. Through the experience, people will contemplate the complementarity between human and technology, and discover their own existence through a dialogue with their artistic selves. The audience will reveal their own voice through the ever-changing figure, allowing themselves to be heard and felt.

Future Development

Add more interaction: When the audience's pitch is higher than 1500 Hz (when you speak, it's usually not higher than 600, the highest human pitch seems to be around 2000), they will have the opportunity to produce lines on the screen to remove their body image. This part of the interaction is designed to present when people express themselves without being too loud and also to keep some space for others to express their ideas. With the explosion of information, it is still important to listen.

Experience Enhancement: Some viewers didn't realize that what was on the screen was themselves. The delay in the model and the various patterns confused them a bit and did not reflect the real-time body shape. The different parts of the visuals could be slightly divided to better make the viewer aware of their presence. Adding a description page/voice over before the start would also be a viable approach, but could potentially ruin the overall experience.
