In collaboration with Tian Qin & Zhao Yang
Responsible for Game Design, Scene Design, UI & UX
Exhibition
NYU Shanghai IMA Show Spring 2021
First CUSGA (China University Student Game Award)
Toolkit
Unity / C# / Photoshop / Blender
*Most models & Music is from the internet.
Ant’s Adventure is a mobile phone third-person perspective flight simulation game. Player will use the mobile phone gyroscope as the medium to control the character to fly over numerous obstacles and pass through different levels to reach the final destination.

Inspiration

Flower by Chen Xinghan

In Flower, the player controls the wind, blowing a flower through the air using the movement of the game controller. Flying close to flowers results in the player's petal being followed by other flower petals. Approaching flowers may also have side-effects on the game world, such as bringing vibrant color to previously dead fields or activating stationary . The game features no text or dialogue, forming a narrative arc primarily through visual representation and emotional cues.

Loner by Kunpo Games

'Loneliness is a kind of beauty, a quiet experience.'
Loner is a very simple game, no attributes, no upgrade, or even no score, what it wants to bring you, is only purely tranquillity and loneliness. It tells the story of a lonely journey of a pilot, in the journey, you would understand his past and his pursuit.

Gameplay & Story

Given the theme "ride", the gameplay is that the player needs to control the ant  to ride the leaf, and to collect as many candies as possible during the journey. To enrich the game background, we set the story as an little ant who can no longer endure the endless work, and one day obtained the crown of courage, with the prayers of his companions ready to go to the outside world to find magic candy, to save the companions who work day after day.

Game Design

To construct a consistent gameplay experience and complete the backstory, we designed four levels set in the four seasons with progressively increasing difficulty and world scale. Using models from the Unity asset store, we redesigned the world scenes and designed the travel paths as best as possible based on difficulty. By placing magic candies in the game, we showed the player the best way forward.


In order to create a better game experience for players, we also designed a series of guides, including but not limited to AI fox guide, which will automatically follow your route and point you to the right path, which is implemented by NavMesh; users will also trigger certain UI notifications in the first level (teaching level) to guide their basic operations.

User Test

During the end-of-semester IMA show, as well as the first Chinese University Student Game Award, we had many players test our game and have collected many useful suggestions. One of the biggest thing to improve is the operating method. Given that we use the phone's built-in gyroscope and all the six directions are activated, the game is not friendly to novices. High-level difficulty makes most people stop at the first two levels and lost interest in exploring further.


It is also interesting that players said they have a better experience in general when looking at a bigger screen and only use phone as a controller. This idea of the players greatly inspired us - the phone can be used not only as a platform, but also as a very convenient and easily accessible controller.
‍

Future Improvement

There are three main directions we want to improve:
1. Difficulty adjustment: As mentioned in the user testing section, the difficulty of this game is not very friendly to first-time players. The learning curve should be smoother. To achieve this, the gyroscope operation and design in each level need to be optimized.
2. Online Mode: Since this is a competitive game and the number of candies/deaths will be recorded, it is better to make the game online/multiplayer to increase the playability of the game.
3. Scene reconstruction: If there is enough time and resources, the production team hopes to rebuild the scene by building their own models and background music, which is what this version lacks.
